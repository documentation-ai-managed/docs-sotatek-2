## Quick Start

Get your project running with SotaTek in a few simple steps.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Sign agreement">
    
    E-sign the contract sent to your registered email.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Join workspace">
    
    Accept your Slack/Teams invite for real-time communication.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Share project files">
    
    Upload initial requirements to the shared drive link provided.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Schedule kickoff call">
    
    Book an intro call with your assigned Project Manager.
  </Step>
</Steps>
