---
title: Cannot Connect With Project Team
description: Resolve issues reaching your SotaTek team.
---

## Symptoms

- No response via email/chat
- Missed meetings

## Quick Fixes

<Steps>
  <Step title="Check contact details">
    Ensure you are using the correct email provided in onboarding.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Try alternative channels">
    Message on Slack or Microsoft Teams as listed in your intro email.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Escalate">
    If no reply within 24 hours, email support@sotatek.com with your project code.
  </Step>
</Steps>
