## Prerequisites

Before contacting SotaTek, prepare your project vision. Outline your business goals, target audience, and technical requirements. Gather any existing documentation or wireframes.

<Callout kind="info">
  Ensure you have:
  - A clear project scope summary
  - Budget estimates
  - Timeline expectations
  - Key stakeholders identified
</Callout>

## Schedule Your Consultation

Once contacted, SotaTek's team responds within 24 hours to schedule an initial 30-minute call.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Respond to Confirmation" icon="check">
    Reply to the scheduling email with your availability.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Join the Call" icon="video">
    Use the provided Zoom or Google Meet link. Prepare to discuss your needs.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Outline Requirements" icon="edit-3">
    Share your project details. Use this structured format for clarity:

    <CodeGroup tabs="JSON,YAML">
```json
{
  "project": "E-commerce Platform",
  "features": ["User authentication", "Payment integration", "Admin dashboard"],
  "techStack": ["React", "Node.js", "PostgreSQL"],
  "timeline": "3 months",
  "budget": "50,000 USD"
}
```
```yaml
project: E-commerce Platform
features:
  - User authentication
  - Payment integration
  - Admin dashboard
techStack:
  - React
  - Node.js
  - PostgreSQL
timeline: 3 months
budget: 50,000 USD
```
    </CodeGroup>
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Sign NDA and Kickoff

After the consultation, sign a non-disclosure agreement (NDA) to protect your ideas.

<Callout kind="tip">
  The NDA ensures confidentiality during discovery and proposal phases. Review it carefully before signing.
</Callout>

SotaTek provides a customized proposal within 48 hours, including scope, timeline, and pricing.
